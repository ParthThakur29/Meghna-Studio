Hi, I'm Ketan Thakur. I've been doing photography since 2000 — that's over 20 years now. Started from Valoda and slowly built this up as a full time thing. Most of my work is weddings, but I also do family shoots, baby showers, naming ceremonies, whatever the occasion.

I try not to be the guy who's always asking you to pose. The best photos happen when people forget the camera is even there. That's what I go for every time.
