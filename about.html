My story

How it all
started

I got my first camera in 2000. Back then it was just a hobby — I used to take photos at relatives' functions and people liked them. Slowly it became a proper profession. I never formally studied photography, mostly self taught and learned from experience over the years.

I've covered weddings, pujas, baby showers, ring ceremonies, family get-togethers — all kinds of events mostly around Valoda, Surat and nearby areas. I try not to make people pose too much. The photos that come out best are usually the ones where no one is even looking at the camera.

It's been 25 years now and I still enjoy it the same way. Every wedding is different and that keeps it interesting for me.

— Ketan Thakur
